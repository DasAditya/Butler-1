What happens when 3 SPIT geeks meet? An amazing company is conceptualized!
Meet our founders - Sanket Agrawal, Aditya Das and Amit Gaikwad
These 3 guys realized that people are spending their precious free time on managing household chores, looking for restaurants instead of pursuing their passions! The problem also existed on service providers' side who had to struggle with minimal training and poor earning potential. Upset but elated, that they had a problem to solve, they set out to create a one stop solution for all household needs. And thus, The Butler was founded in October 2016 with the vision of creating happy households!
